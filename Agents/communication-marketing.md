# Communication Marketing Skill - meet

## Current Position
`meet` is a provisioned app shell. It is not yet a finished meeting product.

## Copy Rules
- Say what exists: React static frontend, BugZero widget, deployment wiring.
- Do not claim scheduling, video conferencing, transcripts, AI notes, reminders, CRM sync, auth, or team workspaces exist until implemented.
- Keep copy direct and grounded.

## Discovery Questions
- Who is the primary user?
- What meeting pain does this solve?
- Is this for scheduling, joining, note-taking, follow-up, facilitation, analytics, or another workflow?
- What is the desired conversion action on the first screen?
